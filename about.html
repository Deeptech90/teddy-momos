Our Story

Born from Passion

Teddy Momos started with a simple idea: to bring the authentic, comforting taste of Himalayan dumplings to our neighborhood. What began as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a beloved local spot for foodies.

Our mascot, Teddy, represents the warmth and friendliness we pour into every dish. We believe food isn't just fuel; it's a memory, a comfort, and a joy.
